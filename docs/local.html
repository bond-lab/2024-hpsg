Local Information

We are looking forward to seeing you in Olomouc!

The meeting will take place at the Corpus Christi Chapel in the Arts Center, Palacký University.

The address is: Univerzitní 225/3, 779 00 Olomouc 9, Czechia

You enter the main door, into a tunnel. Immediately turn left and go up some stairs. The chapel is at the end of the corridor. It will be sign posted.

Getting to Olomouc

The two closest major airports are Prague and Vienna. There is a detailed guide to get to Olomouc from the Sinofon Project.

Other Information

WIFI: Access to eduroam is available. Palacký University belongs to the eduroam initiative and, therefore, enables users of any organization affiliated with the eduroam initiative to access its wireless network. The visiting user must use the eduroam SSID. Before coming and using the wireless network, we recommend that you check that your organization belongs to the eduroam initiative and that you have configured the service on your device.

We can also give access to a guest account if you don't have eduroam.

There is a detailed guide to accommodation in Olomouc from the Sinofon Project.
